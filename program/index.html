Program

This lecture series consists of 12 weeks with two meetings each, these being: an pre-lecture lecture and an application lecture. The first aims to put every participant "in the same page" regarding a prerequisite of the application lecture of the current week. The latter aims to expose and apply an approach based on the contents explored in the pre-lecture to expand our current understanding of Deep Learning methods. The final week will consist of a panel with the lecturers of previous weeks to discuss the frontlines of this new field.

All meetings will be transmited and the links will be posted in this page. Don't worry if you miss any meeting, the recordings will be available at Data ICMC YouTube Channel!

Important:

  • All the pre-lectures (highlighted in red) will be taught in Portuguese
  • While all the application lectures (highlighted in blue) will be taught in English
  • The full schedule of this page is in the São Paulo, Brazil (GMT -3:00) time zone, which can be quickly converted here
